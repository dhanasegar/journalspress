Our Mission

Educore Journals is dedicated to the global dissemination of high-quality research across all academic disciplines. We provide a platform for researchers to share their findings with the world, fostering innovation and collaboration.

{% endcomment %}

Aim & Scope

Our aim is to provide a rigorous peer-reviewed platform for researchers to publish innovative work that advances their fields of study. We welcome submissions across all academic disciplines.

The scope of Educore Journals encompasses original research articles, review papers, case studies, and short communications that demonstrate academic excellence and contribute meaningfully to the body of knowledge.

  • Open access to all published content
  • Rapid peer-review process
  • Global editorial board
  • No publication fees for authors
